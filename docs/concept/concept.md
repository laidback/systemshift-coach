## Positioning

Systemic coaching for IT professionals. Not generic life coaching — specifically for senior devs, tech leads, engineering managers, and CTOs who are technically excellent but stuck in systemic loops: bad org dynamics, burnout, career ceilings, work-life collision.

**Unique angle:** Lukas is both — 20+ years as an IT professional (developer, tech lead, engineering manager) *and* a trained systemic coach. He knows how IT organisations work from the inside.

**Target ICP:**
- Senior Developers (5–15 yrs exp)
- Tech Leads
- Engineering Managers
- CTOs / VP Engineering
- Feeling stuck, burned out, or blocked despite being good at their job

## Coaching Approach

**Systemic.** Not the individual alone — the system around them. Relationships, structures, hidden rules, dynamics. Symptoms are signals of systemic patterns.

**No quick-fix.** Real change takes time and honest reflection. Not motivational speaking, not 10-step frameworks, not consulting disguised as coaching.

**Direct.** Like the IT world Lukas came from: specific, concrete, no waffle.

## AI Layer — CoachBot

Private coaching assistant for Lukas only (not client-facing).

- Session prep: client context, systemic hypothesis, success definition
- Session notes: capture patterns, shifts (anonymised — no real client names)
- Follow-up drafts: DE/EN, brief, professional
- Resource bank: frameworks, models, quotes tagged by theme
- End-of-day reflection prompts

See `/agent/` for CoachBot spec.
