I work with IT leaders, senior engineers, and tech professionals at inflection points.

That might mean: you've just moved into a leadership role and discovered it's a completely different discipline. Or you're a senior individual contributor wondering what's next and why. Or you're inside an organisation that's gotten complex enough to feel stuck, even though you can see exactly what's wrong.

I bring two things to this work:

20+ years inside technology organisations. I'm not learning your context from scratch — I already understand the landscape: the sprint rhythms, the architecture debates, the gap between technical excellence and organisational effectiveness.

Systemic coaching methodology (ISTOB C25). Systemic coaching is evidence-based, non-advisory, and built on the premise that the person in the room already has what they need — the coach's job is to help them access it. No frameworks to fill in. No advice unless you ask for it.

I work with people who want to think more clearly about their situation — and find their own next move.

Discovery calls are free and without obligation. If it's a fit, we'll know quickly.
