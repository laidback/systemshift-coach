## About

I spent 20+ years inside technology organisations — network engineering at Telekom scale, backend development, DevOps, Kubernetes, system and software architecture. I know what it feels like to be the person in the room who actually understands how it works. And I know what happens when that stops being enough.

That's why I became a systemic coach.

**systemshift.coach** is my practice. I work with IT professionals, tech leads, and senior engineers who are navigating complexity — in their organisations, their careers, or themselves.

This isn't generic coaching with an IT veneer. I bring two things together that rarely travel together:

- Systemic coaching methodology (ISTOB C25 — structured, evidence-based)
- 20+ years inside tech — sprints, on-call, architecture reviews, reorgs, the works

If you're technically excellent and finding that's not enough anymore — that's a systems problem. That's where I work.

**What I work on with clients:**
- Tech leads stepping into leadership for the first time
- Senior engineers at a crossroads — what's next, and why
- IT professionals carrying too much, with no clear lever
- CTOs and tech founders whose organisations are scaling faster than their leadership capacity

**Format:** 1:1 sessions, online, German or English
**Package:** 6 sessions · 130 €/hour

If this sounds like the conversation you've been putting off — let's have it.
